      Help!


      Here are a few basic tips to help you get the most out of your Weebly experience:

      • Most things can be dragged around to where you want them.
      • Most things can be double-clicked to edit them.
      • You add content to your page by expanding the Content Menu ("Add Content +") and dragging the element you want on to the middle area.
      • Your pages are listed on the left hand side. Click a page to edit that page, drag it up or down to re-order it, and double click it to edit its settings.
      • To remove an element, drag it towards the trash can until it "compacts".
      • To publish your website, click "Publish web site". From here, you'll be able to choose where your site will be located (ie yourpage.weebly.com), and other site settings.
      • Publishing generally takes less than 15 seconds. Once your site it published, it will be immediately available.

      If there's anything that's difficult to use or generally not intuitive, please leave us feedback and let us know!
